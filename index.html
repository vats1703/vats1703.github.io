"Short" Bio

I'm Victor Torres, or simply Alex to most. Rooted in the South-American coastal city of Machala, Ecuador, my formative years were shaped by endless summers, a military high school, and countless swimming sessions surrounded by vast banana fields and the sea on the other side.

Fueling my journey was an innate curiosity, leading me to a profound love for Physics. This passion guided me to Yachay Tech University, where, diverging from potential paths in the Air Force or Petroleum engineering, I immersed myself in a physics degree. Five transformative years unfolded, filled with lectures, research projects, and a gravitational pull towards the captivating world of astrophysics, gravitational waves and data analysis.

A brief year working as a manager assistant preceed my Italian chapter. Securing a scholarship, I pursued a Master's degree in Physics, navigating between the germanic charm of Trento at its university, and the scenic Adriatic shores of Trieste at SISSA. This enriching experience extended my exploration of gravity waves and data analysis, laying the foundation for applying my acquired skills to diverse industrial challenges.
